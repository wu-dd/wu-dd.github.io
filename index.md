## About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of Tokyo, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. <a href="http://www.ms.k.u-tokyo.ac.jp/sugi/index.html" style="text-decoration: none;">Masashi Sugiyama</a>. I am also a Junior Research Associate at the <a href="https://aip.riken.jp/labs/generic_tech/imperfect_inf_learn" style="text-decoration: none;">Imperfect Information Learning Team</a>, RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (RIKEN AIP). Previously, I received my M.Eng. degree from Southeast University under the supervision of Prof. <a href="http://palm.seu.edu.cn/zhangml" style="text-decoration: none;">Min-Ling Zhang</a>. I also spent a wonderful time as a Research Assistant at MBZUAI, advised by Prof. <a href="https://zhiqiangshen.com/" style="text-decoration: none;">Zhiqiang Shen</a>. Earlier in my research journey, I interned at Ant Group and CASIA.


## Research Interests

My research focuses mainly on weakly supervised learning (for classification, generation, and LLM post-training), with broader interests in efficient learning (for agents, LLMs, and data) and AI safety.
